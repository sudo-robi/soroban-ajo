## Tips

### Profile Customization
1. Add a display name to personalize your profile
2. Write a bio to introduce yourself to the community
3. Add your email to receive important notifications

### Privacy Settings
- Toggle "Show Profile" to control visibility
- Disable "Show Activity" to keep transactions private
- Turn off "Show Statistics" to hide your stats

### Notifications
- Enable "Payout Reminders" to never miss a payout
- Turn on "Contribution Reminders" to stay on track
- Customize which notifications you receive
